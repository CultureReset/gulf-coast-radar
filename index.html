What is Gulf Coast Radar?

Gulf Coast Radar is the AI-powered discovery platform for Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, and surrounding areas. We help tourists find restaurants, activities, and events instantly—and we help businesses get discovered by thousands of visitors every day.

With our voice-powered AI concierge, complete digital profiles, and real-time event listings, we're making it easier than ever to explore the Gulf Coast. Every business gets their own AI assistant that knows their menu, specials, and hours—ready to help customers 24/7.
