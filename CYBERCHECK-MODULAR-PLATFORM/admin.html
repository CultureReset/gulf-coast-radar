Bulk Events

Upload Events CSV

Upload events for multiple businesses at once. System matches events to businesses by name.

Old DB Columns:
business_name, event_name, description, date, start_time, end_time, price, image_url
GCR Event Columns:
business_name, artist_name, date, start_time, end_time, description, price_text, image_url, link_url

Notes:
• business_name matches by name OR slug
• end_time is optional — leave blank if no end time
• date format: 2026-05-15 or May 15 or 05/15/2026
• start_time / end_time: 8:00 PM or 20:00

Example:
Cobalt The Restaurant, DJ Mike, 2026-05-15, 8:00 PM, 11:00 PM, Live DJ on the patio, Free, https://...,
Tee Off at The Wharf, Trivia Night, 2026-05-20, 7:00 PM, , Weekly bar trivia, Free, ,
